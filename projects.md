## Featured Public Project

### Groundhog — Synthetic Network Traffic Generator (Hackathon Template)

**Goal:** Provide on-demand synthetic traffic generation for realistic network test scenarios.

**Stack:** AWS, RabbitMQ, Python, automation workflows.

**Highlights:**

- Placed 3rd out of 20+ teams in internal company competition.
